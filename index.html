FAQ

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a event where passionate student with common interest come together to create and build something amazing.
What should I bring?
Attendee must bring a vaild school ID or governemnt ID for admission. Obviously, bring your laptop, charger and any snacks so you don't be hungry during your hacking.
Who can attend?
Any college student, undergraduate graduate can attend. Whether you never coded before, currently playing around with photoshop or creating your first 3D model are welcome to join us.
Does it cost anything?
Why would anyone pay to suffer for years in one room? So yeah, nope! Admissions is free for anyone, which includes food, drinks and snacks. However, we won't be providing any sort of transportation.
How does teams work?
Teams may conatin up to 4 people. If you don't have a team, you can still apply, but you will be grouped up with others we don't also have a team at the beginning of the hackathon. If you want to form a team you can join our discord ACM server and in the #team-finder channel you can send a message to see who's willing to collaborate for you.
Will food be provided?
Yes, definitely! Food will be provided during the whole event. It's recommend you also bring your own snacks during the hacking. If you have any dietary restrictions, it's highly recommend you bring your own food.
Still have questions?
Feel free to send us a email at acm.calstatela@gmail.com or direct message from any board members from our discord server.
